Hi, my name is Allison and I have some advice for you!

Setting Aside Time

It is important to have a schedualed time to sit down and work. Even though this is self paced, look at it as if you are taking online classes through a University. It should hold the same importance. I tell friends that I cannot do anything at a certain time because I am taking my online classes.

Learn To Explain Your Thinking

Being able to explain your thinking while coding is important for your future career. Employers want to understand your why you are making the decisions you are making. Being able to explain your thinking will also help you be able to communicate with you team when there is confusion or issues with the code.

When You Get Stuck

First, check for typos. If there are no typos, check your notes to make sure your code is correct. If you are still having trouple ask someone for help. Make sure you tell them what you have already done to try to fix the issue.
