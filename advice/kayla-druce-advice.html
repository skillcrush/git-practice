Break it down by time!

When you first start coding, it can be quite overwhelming. I found it useful to use the pomodoro method. I used a free app on my phone to break down time into 25 min blocks of focus plus 5 minute block of break-time. For every 25 minute pomodoro I completed, I'd give myself a sticker on a peice of paper I was turning into a picture with a quote. This helped me see my progress even when it got challenging. I aim to atleast get one sticker per day, or never skip two days in a row, to remain consistent.
