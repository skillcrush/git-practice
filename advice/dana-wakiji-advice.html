Hi, my name is Dana and I have some advice for you!

Patience is a virtue.

That is an old saying but it is repeated so often because it is true.

It definitely applies when you are first learning to code. You have to take your time so you can absorb all the information.

Write things down.

When learning something new, it often helps to write things down on paper. That can help you remember and you can also refer back to it when you need to.

Be kind to yourself.

Don't beat yourself up when you make mistakes as they're bound to happen.

It isn't fun but it is part of the learning process and will make you better in the long rum.

If you get frustrated, take a break. Maybe go for a walk, make a cup of coffee or tea or listen to your favorite podcast. When you return, your mind will be refreshed and you can dive back in with a new attitude.
