Hi, my name is Cesar and I have some advice for you!

Be Curious

Being curious while learning something new like coding will deepen your understanding throughout your Skillcrush classes. Follow those instructions in front of you because surely they have been carefully planned for you. Be curious to ask yourself why that code or command works, but don't dwell on it too long because your answer will arrive soon enough.

There is more than one way to code

This is true! I learned this along the way. How, you ask? I was curious enough to experiment during my lessons. However, be humble when your way is not the right way and learn from your mistakes. You will be full of wisdom on this journey.

Find Your Pace

Everyone has a different learning pace so find yours and stick with it.
