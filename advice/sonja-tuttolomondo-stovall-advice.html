Practice Helps!

There are so many different commands and processes to learn while coding. Keep practicing and you will get it. The steps will become more memorable.

When practice is not helping, look to the resources available to you.

Don't be afraid to search tthe internet, or look at the many resources available to you. This isn't cheating, this is being smart.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.
