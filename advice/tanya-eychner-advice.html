Hi, my name is Tanya and I have some advice for you!

Believe in yourself!

The first thing to do is believe that you deserve to be here, learning something new, taking on a fantastic challenge, and opening up a whole world of new opportunities! It may seem overwhelming at times, especially when previewing each new task ahead, but you have been given the tools that it takes to complete the task and impress yourself every time.

Trust the process.

As you go through the process of learning something new, you may not be able to see how it all comes together, or how it will work in the real world...that's okay! Trust that you are on track to make the changes to your life and career that taking these steps are preparing you for.

As you move deeper into this new language, you are learning what it takes to break into tech. Do the work, learn the steps and visualize your success. You've GOT this!
