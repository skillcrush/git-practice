Incase anyone reads this, I'm Jared and I hope these words help you on your journey!

Patience is the greatest virtue!

When you start anything new and frustration kicks in,
just take a breath, relax and remember that patience is a virtue.

You are more likely to solve a mentally taxing problem when you are in a relaxed frame of mind,
not a stressed and panicked state.

Let those critical thoughts or doubts be.

Most of us, if not all of us get them and they are normal. The problems come when we listen to them or buy into them.
Don't bother trying to combat them, just let them be there and continue on.

Relative paths work better here!
