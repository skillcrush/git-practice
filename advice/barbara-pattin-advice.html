Hi, I'm Barbara. Here are my tips for you about learning to code!

Comparing yourself to others is a waste of time

Just don't. Stay focused on your goals, your accomplishments and why you want to learn to code. Keep a journal as a reminder of how far you've come.

If you're having a tough day, step away. Take a walk, call a friend. Do something that makes you happy and then come back with fresh eyes.

Find your tribe

Surround yourself with positive people and a supportive community. There's always someone out there who can help and wants to.

Remember to thank those who offer answers. Don't forget to give back generously to the group when you're further along your path.

Listen to podcasts, watch videos, and read about how others have learned to code for inspiration. Follow, like and share resources you find.

You don't need to know "all the things"...

if you know where to find the answers and how to search! Learn and apply new skills as you go.

Be confident that you'll find the solution. Don't be embarassed to call on your tribe and ask for help.

Keep showing up and working at it.
