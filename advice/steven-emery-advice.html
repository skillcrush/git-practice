Hi, my name is Steve and I have some advice for you!

Look ahead

Whatever coding you are learning now, even if it looks hazy and fuzzy, persevere as it'll become clearer in the long term.

It is normal to feel overwhelmed, confused, impatient, but give it time and you'll get those 'ah' moments.

Persevere

Don't give up! Review the code, look for comma's, full points, capital letters, sometimes it's just a typo!

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

Take breaks, take a look through other learning resources, or go back and start at scratch with a project or code you are working with! In the long run everything clicks, so don't give up!
