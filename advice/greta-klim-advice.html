Hi, my name is Greta and I have some advice for you!

Discipline is KEY!

It's motivating to start something new. But, the excitement of a new adventure soon loses its luster when its time to work hard. My advice to you is to KEEP GOING!

  • Step one: create a routine that most excites you.
  • Step Two: Show up every day at that time to singularly focus on the task at hand.
  • Step Three: Seek wisdom with the resources available through the program like Skillcrush Student Slack
  • Step four: Keep at it! Every day, even when you feel unmotivated, persist! Motivation will come in due time.

By following these steps, you will begin to develop belief in yourself. Great job!

Its simple, but not easy.

S When learning a new skill, always speak to yourself kindly. Develop your patience and curiosity when you encounter errors. Develop a healthy strategy for when you are feeling disregulated or upset.

Thank goodness for the teachers of Skillcrush. They have made it simple for us to learn. However, that doesnt mean the road ahead is without difficulty. Keep a steady pace and a cool head.

Lastly, have fun, be creative and get going! You have the power to make it happen!
