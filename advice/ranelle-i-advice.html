Hi, my name is Ranelle, and I have some advice for you!

Embrace the beginning

You're just starting on your journey, and once you've learned these skills, you'll never be a beginner again. There's something really beautiful about knowing that, and I want you to embrace it. Ask questions. Find ways to learn what you don't know. Get curious.

Mindset is key

By committing to this program, you are committing to a growth mindset. Frustration is expected when things go awry. Take time to decompress if needed; then form a plan to overcome the obstacles ahead of you. You will improve quickly when you can take your failures, mistakes, and setbacks and use them as motivation to move forward.
