Just Start.

Don't wait for the perfect moment to begin your new path - just start.


If you need a sign, this is it. Put the money on the barrelhead and just go for it.

Tell someone.

Maybe it's your mom, maybe it's your best friend, or perhaps it's your dog. Just tell someone, anyone. If someone else knows you're working on a goal it just makes it that much easier to keep working.


Don't worry if it's not perfect.

You've got plenty of time to learn more and keep going.
