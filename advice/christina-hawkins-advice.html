Hi, my name is Christina and I have some advice for you!

Get Comfortable with the Uncomfortable

Whether you're learning a new hobbie, picking up a long-past interest, or developing an entirely new skill like coding - You are bound to feel uncomfortable. This discomfort is all a part of newness - Keep going!

Get Messy

Don't be afraid of making mistakes, of troubleshooting on your own, or researching more information outside of your class resources. The sooner, and more often, you stand on your own feet the better you will be while working on your own or as a part of a team.

Get Bold

Lastly, loose all fear of asking for help. Beyond all of the resources at your disposal, your fellow coders are a wealth of knowledge and are incredibly helpful. Find a coding community on twitter, facebook, and other of your favorite social networks. You'll be suprised how many are out there and are willing to through out ideas when you're stuck!
