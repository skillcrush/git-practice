Hi, my name is Abigail and I have some advice for you!

Just Do Your Best!

When you start learning how to code, it seems like you're learning a new language. That's because you are! Don't expect to fully understand everything on the first pass. Just do your best to understand core concepts. Don't be afraid to go through a course more than once if you need to!

Stay Consistent

One of the best things you can do while learning how to code is to stay consistent. If you go weeks between lessons, you'll lose interest and your skills won't remain as sharp. When you do something consistently and show up for yourself, your ability to learn and grasp new concepts expands. Your brain and skills stay flexibile and it's much easier to continue learning new things!

Be Patient

Don't beat yourself up. Think of the type of teacher you'd want and be that for yourself. Kind, gentle, forgiving, and encouraging. If you don't get something the first time, that's okay! Keep working at it. Looking up resources outside of Skillcrush or using other tools in your toolbelt (like your instructors and classmates) is a great resource.
