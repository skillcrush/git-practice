Hi, my name is Sierra and I have some tips that have helped me along the way!

Remember, it is a huge accomplishment to be learning something new!

Development is hard, its hard for everyone at first. Dont give up, find new ways to stay engaged and motivated during this journey.

Even "real" programmers Google things every day!

Dont be afraid to ask for help. Get comforatble researching and studying additional resources on the web. There is a lot of information out there that can aid you when your stuck. Sometimes taking a break for a few hours or even a day, can help you get a fresh perspective. Just trust that in time you will understand how to do more, and programming will start to feel more natural.
