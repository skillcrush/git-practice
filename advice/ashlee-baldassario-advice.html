Hi, my name is Ashlee and I have some advice for you!

Practice, Practice, Practice!

Like any new skill, programming and coding take practice. From what I have learned so far, the practice never ends, even when you are a certified coding guru! There will also always be new tricks and languages as technology advances.

I like to compare it to yoga. There is always something to improve upon or learn and the continual practice is part of the journey.

Making mistakes happens to everyone.

There will be mistakes, no questions asked. No matter how versed you are in a language or program, something as simple as a typo can create turmoil within the project. Do not fret! Work with your team, coworkers, Skillcrushers alum and Github community to help you verify the errors.

There is endless support out there and all at the press of a button.
