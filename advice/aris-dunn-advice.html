Hi, my name is Aris Dunn and I have some advice for you!

Persistance is your friend!

It can get frustrating at times, but don't give up. Take a break. Walk away. Ask an instructor or brainstorm with your fellow Skillcrushers. Then go back and try again!

Immerse yourself.

Read blogs, listen to podcasts, study resources and read code. Most of it will look and sound like Greek at first, but you'll be encouraged to find yourself understanding more as you go along.
