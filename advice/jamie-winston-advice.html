Hi, my name is Jamie Winston and I have some advice for you!

Be Shaken but Not Stirred!

You will face challenges but don't let them make you a quitter. If there is something you don't know it just means that you have more to learn and you are fully capable of doing so!

When you feel overwhelmed by a challenge do the following:

  1. Take a break (go for a walk, stretch, put in that load of laundry, etc.)
  2. Start from the beginning and mentally or actively walk through each step again. Cover your bases!
  3. Check for those small errors (grammar, punctuation, curly quotes ugghh!, etc.)

Pause but Don't Stop

You're learning A LOT and the best way to retain this information is to practice using it. Stepping away from it for too long can lead to you forgetting information.

If you need to lessen your pace then go slower but KEEP GOING!
